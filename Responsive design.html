I was born and raised in the city of Toronto. I have traveled to many vacations spots in the Carribean and across The United States of America. When I was a child I spent alot of time drawing and playing with computer programs. I believe my intrest in computer software began very early in life. In school we had the option to order from scholastic book club. In the order guide there were books that had line of code in them that would create a computer game if you had enough patience to log them in. Once when my cousin had stayed with me during a summer holiday for 5 weeks, he programmed a racecar game from skratch that took what seemed like forever but when it was finished we played that game until we wore it out. That experience changed me . Learning that if you understood how to program a computer you could get it to do things.
